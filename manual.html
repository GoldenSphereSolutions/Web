ZzzCatcher User Manual

Everything you need to know to get the most out of your sleep monitoring.

1. Set Your Trigger Threshold

ZzzCatcher uses two methods to trigger recordings: Volume-based (when sounds cross into the red zone on the noise bar) and Smart Snore Detection (advanced AI). Use the slider on the main screen to set a baseline volume, and ensure 'Smart Detection' is enabled in settings for the most accurate triggers.

2. Choose Your Mode

Select whether you want to record Audio Only or Video & Audio Pro. Tap the large START button to begin monitoring.

Pro Tips for Night Video:
  • Use the Back Camera: It is much more sensitive to light than the front camera.
  • Positioning: Place your phone 2-3 feet from a wall so the 'Night Light' can bounce off it and softly illuminate the room.
  • External Light: For the clearest video, we recommend a dim, comfortable lamp or night light in your room.

3. Blackout Mode

Once monitoring has started, tap the Moon icon to darken your screen. This helps you sleep better while the app continues to monitor in the background. To exit Blackout Mode, simply double-tap anywhere on the black screen.

4. Nightly Summary & Sharing Pro

View the Nightly Summary Dashboard to see total sleep time, snore counts, and abnormal events. Use the Share button to export recordings and event logs to your doctor or sleep specialist.

5. Battery & Power Management

Monitoring sleep (especially in Video Mode) can consume significant battery power. To ensure uninterrupted recording throughout the night, we strongly recommend keeping your device plugged into a charger. If your battery level falls too low, iOS may automatically terminate background apps to preserve power.

6. Privacy & Automatic Stop

To protect your privacy, ZzzCatcher will automatically stop monitoring after 12 hours. Monitoring will also pause automatically if you receive a phone call. Free users can access the last 3 days of recordings, while Pro users enjoy a 30-day history.

Medical Disclaimer: ZzzCatcher is for informational purposes only and is not a medical device. It cannot diagnose Sleep Apnea or any other medical condition. If you are concerned about your sleep health, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
