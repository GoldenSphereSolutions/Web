ZzzCatcher User Manual

Everything you need to know to get the most out of your sleep monitoring.

1. Set Your Trigger Threshold

ZzzCatcher uses two methods to trigger recordings: 1) Volume-based (when sounds cross into the red zone on the noise bar) and 2) Smart Snore Detection (advanced AI). Use the slider to set a baseline volume, and enable 'Smart Detection' in settings for even more accurate triggers that ignore movement and yawning.

2. Choose Your Mode & Start

Select whether you want to record Audio Only or Video & Audio Pro. Tap the large START button to begin monitoring.

PRO TIPS FOR NIGHT VIDEO:
  • Use the Back Camera: It is much more sensitive to light than the front camera.
  • Positioning: Place your phone 2-3 feet from a wall so the 'Night Light' can bounce off the wall and illuminate the room.
  • External Light: For the clearest video, we recommend a very dim, comfortable night light or lamp in your room.

3. Blackout Mode for Better Sleep

Once monitoring has started, tap the Moon icon to darken your screen. The app continues to listen in the background. To exit Blackout Mode, simply double-tap anywhere on the black screen.

4. Nightly Summary & Sharing Pro

With ZzzCatcher Pro, you get a full Nightly Summary Dashboard that calculates total sleep time, snore counts, and abnormal events. You also unlock Video + Audio recording mode and can use the Share button to export recordings and event logs to your doctor or sleep specialist.

5. Battery & Power Management

ZzzCatcher (especially in Video Mode) can consume significant battery. To ensure uninterrupted monitoring all night, we strongly recommend keeping your device plugged into a charger. If the battery falls below a critical level, iOS may automatically stop the app to preserve power.

6. Privacy & Automatic Stop

To preserve your device's battery and protect your daytime privacy, ZzzCatcher will automatically stop monitoring after 12 hours. Monitoring will also automatically pause if you receive a phone call.

7. Manage History & Storage

Free users can access the last 3 days of recordings, while Pro users enjoy a 30-day history. You can manually delete recordings in the History view. We recommend keeping at least 10GB of free space on your device for optimal performance.

8. Smart Detection & Abnormal Events

Smart Snore Detection is now free for all users to ensure high-accuracy recordings. When enabled, a 'SMART AI' badge will appear, and a 'SNORE DETECTED' indicator will show when the AI identifies a snore. ZzzCatcher Pro adds advanced detection for abnormal sounds like gasping or coughing, automatically flagging those recordings with an '⚠️' icon in your history.
