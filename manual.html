ZzzCatcher User Manual

Everything you need to know to get the most out of your sleep monitoring.

1. Set Your Trigger Threshold

ZzzCatcher uses two methods to trigger recordings: Volume-based (when sounds cross into the red zone on the noise bar) and Smart Snore Detection (advanced AI). Use the slider on the main screen to set a baseline volume, and ensure 'Smart Detection' is enabled in settings for the most accurate triggers.

2. Choose Your Mode

Select whether you want to record Audio Only or Video & Audio Pro. Tap the large START button to begin monitoring. If camera access is denied or Pro is not active, the app will automatically fall back to Audio Only mode.

Pro Tip: In very dark rooms, recorded video may be dark. For the best results, enable Night Light in settings and use the Back Camera (it is more sensitive to light than the front camera).

3. Blackout Mode

Once monitoring has started, tap the Moon icon to darken your screen. This helps you sleep better while the app continues to monitor in the background. To exit Blackout Mode, simply double-tap anywhere on the black screen.

4. Nightly Summary & Sharing Pro

View the Nightly Summary Dashboard to see total sleep time, snore counts, and abnormal events. Use the Share button to export recordings and event logs to your doctor or sleep specialist.

5. Smart AI Detection

Smart Snore Detection is active for all users. ZzzCatcher Pro adds advanced detection for abnormal sounds like gasping or coughing, automatically flagging those recordings with a warning icon in your history.

6. Manage History

Free users can access the last 3 days of recordings, while Pro users enjoy a 30-day history. We recommend keeping at least 10GB of free space on your device for optimal performance. Recordings stop automatically after 30 seconds of silence.
