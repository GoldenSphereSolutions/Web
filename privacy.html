Privacy Policy for ZzzCatcher

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Golden Sphere Solutions LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ZzzCatcher mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have full control over your data.

1. Data Collection: Microphone and Camera Access

ZzzCatcher requires access to your device's microphone to monitor and record sleep sounds. If you use the Pro version's Video Monitoring feature, the App also requires access to your device's camera. This access is used solely to record your sleep patterns as requested by you.

2. Local Storage and Data Security

All audio/video recordings and analysis data are processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers. Your recordings never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share a file yourself.

3. Data Sharing

Because your data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or audio/video data to any third parties.

4. User Control and Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. You can delete individual recordings directly within the App. Because all data is stored locally, simply deleting the ZzzCatcher app from your device will permanently erase all associated recordings and data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
